# Chapter 5: Conclusion and Open Humanities Infrastructure

This chapter synthesizes the dissertation's empirical findings, restates the central contribution to literary historiography, and explains the value of the project as reusable research infrastructure.

## Core Claim To Consolidate
The dissertation should conclude that diachronic changes in English poetic prosody between 1850 and 1950 can be modeled as measurable structural adaptation rather than as a purely impressionistic narrative of stylistic change.
